Psychometric Test


  • Imagine you are juggling multiple tasks while using the app. How comfortable would you feel navigating through the features?



  • Choose the color combination that is most appealing and easy for you to distinguish.



  • Select the font size that you find most comfortable for reading text on a screen.



  • When exploring an app, do you prefer fewer options with detailed information or more options with concise information?



  • How quickly can you locate and understand information on a busy screen?



  • How would you like to receive notifications about important updates or events within the app?



  • If you were to prioritize tasks within the app, what would be your preferred method?



  • Adjusting settings for touchscreen sensitivity can enhance your interaction. What level of sensitivity do you find most comfortable?



  • How would you prefer to provide feedback on your experience with the app?
